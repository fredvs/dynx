Here how to do to dynamically load Xlib and Xftlib with fpGUI.

1) Copy all files of /dynx/fpc in the directory of your project.

2) Add in uses section of your program:

  uses fpg_dynload, ...

3) Add this just before fpgApplication.Initialize:
  
  --> fpg_loaddynlib();
 
4) Add in directory search path for compiler:

  /thedirof/dyn_x11 and /thedirof/dyn_pthread

5) Compile your program.
