Here how to do to dynamically load Xlib and Xftlib with fpGUI.

1) Add in uses section of your program:

  uses fpg_dynload, ...

2) Add this just before fpgApplication.Initialize:
  
  --> fpg_loaddynlib();
 
3) Add in directory search path for compiler:

  /thefpguidir/src/corelib/x11/dyn_x11

4) Uncomment in /thefpguidir/src/corelib/x11/fpg_x11.pas :
   
 --->  {$Define DYNLOAD} 

5) Compile your program.
